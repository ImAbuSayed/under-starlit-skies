[Hook]
I can feel the heat from across the room
Like a midnight fire starting to bloom
Every time you look at me that way
There is nothing left for me to say

[Verse 1]
We took the long road out of town
With the windows open and the top rolled down
Now the world is quiet and the shadows play
And I am hoping that you want to stay

Our shadows dancing along the street

[Chorus]

You are the only dream I want the most
